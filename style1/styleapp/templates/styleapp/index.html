ABOUT US

LIVEWIRE - a division of CADD Centre Training Services Pvt Ltd, is a well established and innovative training organization, servicing customers across the country. LIVEWIRE have a peerless engagement model, that seeks to balance, students need with industries requirements. With a plethora of cutting edge courses in our kitty, we deliver value to individuals and companies alike with skill development expertise. LIVEWIRE has training centres in 3 outstanding states in India – Tamil Nadu, Kerala and Karnataka. LIVEWIRE ’s training solutions in IT, ITeS, Engineering Design and Process Management, (EDO/EPO) Knowledge Process Outsourcing (KPO), Business Process Management (BPM), IT Service Management (ITSM) and Executive Management skill enhancement has imparted over thousands of learners since inception. LIVEWIRE’s education process management, curriculum and product development, instructional excellence, and training delivery is well known and that made LIVEWIRE as the most preferred training partner for over thousands of learners. LIVEWIRE hopes to be among the leading training organizations in the world within this decade.
